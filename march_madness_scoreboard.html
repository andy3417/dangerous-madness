How Points Work

Points = Round Multiplier + Seed of Winning Team

Every correct pick earns the round multiplier plus the seed of the team you picked to win. Higher seeds = bigger upsets = more points. A 16-seed Cinderella run is worth massive points!

Round of 64×1 + seed
Round of 32×2 + seed
Sweet 16×4 + seed
Elite 8×8 + seed
Final Four×16 + seed
Championship×32 + seed

Example: You pick a #12 seed to win in the Round of 64 and they do — that's 1 + 12 = 13 pts. Same #12 seed advances and wins the Round of 32 — 2 + 12 = 14 pts. Compare to a boring #1 seed win in the Championship: 32 + 1 = 33 pts vs a #5 seed: 32 + 5 = 37 pts.
